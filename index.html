How We Engineer Happiness

Step 1:

Create a Connection

We connect with our customers every day to ensure we create solutions that deliver on their precise business needs.

Step 2:

Ship Fast, Learn Fast

We believe fiercely that simpler is better. We start by building the simplest solution that will resolve the problem, ship it quickly, and learn based on feedback from our customers. We have a bias for action in all that we do.

Step 3:

Iterate, Iterate, Iterate

Take that simple solution and optimize it for long-term impact, ease of use, and maintainability based on feedback. We value progress over perfection and know we can learn faster when we take small steps and correct course along the way.

Step 4:

Feel the Love

Build your code in the morning, deploy it in the afternoon and bask in the customer love by the end of the day. We make magic for our customers and they love us for it.
